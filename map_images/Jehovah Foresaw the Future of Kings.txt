TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | DANIEL 10-12
Jehovah Foresaw the Future of Kings


11:2
Four kings would stand up for Persia. The fourth would “rouse up everything against the kingdom of Greece.”
	1	Cyrus the Great
	2	Cambyses II
	3	Darius I
	4	Xerxes I (believed to be King Ahasuerus, who married Esther)
11:3
A powerful king of Greece would arise and rule an extensive empire.
	•	Alexander the Great
11:4
The Greek Empire would be divided among four of Alexander’s generals.
	1	Cassander
	2	Lysimachus
	3	Seleucus I
	4	Ptolemy I
